How It Started

Our Dream is Global Learning Transformation

Kanwi was founded by Robert Anderson, a passionate lifelong learner, and Maria Sanchez, a visionary educator. Their shared dream was to create a digital haven of knowledge accessible to all. United by their belief in the transformational power of education, they embarked on a journey to build Kanwi. With dedicated researchers, they gathered a team of experts and launched this innovative platform, creating a global community of eager learners, all connected by the desire to explore, learn, and grow.
