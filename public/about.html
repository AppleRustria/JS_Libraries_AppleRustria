How It Started

Our Dream is Global Learning Transformation

AR/Ture Furniture was founded by Apple Rustria, a visionary designer, and Grace Rustria, a passionate advocate for innovation in home decor. Their shared dream was to create a digital space where cutting-edge design meets accessibility for all. Driven by their belief in the power of technology to transform how we experience furniture, they embarked on a journey to build AR/Ture. With a team of skilled artisans, technologists, and researchers, they launched this forward-thinking platform. AR/Ture Furniture brings together a global community of design enthusiasts, all united by a love for stylish living spaces and the endless possibilities of augmented reality. Together, they are reimagining the way we see, design, and experience furniture in the modern world.
